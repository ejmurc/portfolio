Welcome! This website contains a selection of personal projects and technical write-ups, with the intention of centralizing and documenting my achievements and learnings.

The first of my writings will be here, concisely, to explain the intention behind the website's styling. As I am often prone to spending too much time perfecting a single component of a project and therefore never releasing it, I've made the decision to create a minimal stylesheet that is intentionally "un-designed" but readable, with inspiration taken from LWN.net.

The stylesheet and all website content can be found here. While there was some intention behind the design decisions for the bundling process, it was straightforward to set up and beneficial in the long term: all content is to be served in the most efficient way possible, because why not.
